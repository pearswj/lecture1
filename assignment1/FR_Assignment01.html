- The Milky Way -

The Milky Way is the galaxy that contains the solar system. Moreover, the galaxy's definition is perceived as a "band of light seen in the night sky" - definition from the earth. The term "Milky Way" originates from the Latin word "via lactea" that appears as a band due to its disk-shape structure.
